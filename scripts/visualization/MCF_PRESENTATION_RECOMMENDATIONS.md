### **Typography Standards**

**Slide Titles:**
- Font: Arial or Calibri Bold
- Size: **44-48pt** (matching our visualizations)
- Color: #2C3E50 (dark gray-blue)
- Alignment: Left

**Body Text:**
- Font: Arial or Calibri Regular
- Size: **32-36pt** (minimum 32pt per your requirement)
- Color: #34495E (medium gray)
- Line spacing: 1.5

**Bullet Points:**
- Font: Arial or Calibri Regular
- Size: **32pt**
- Use numbered lists where sequence matters
- Maximum 5-6 bullets per slide

**Labels/Captions:**
- Font: Arial or Calibri
- Size: **28-32pt**
- Color: #7F8C8D (light gray)

**Important Numbers/Stats:**
- Font: Arial Bold
- Size: **48-64pt**

### **Layout Standards**

**Slide Dimensions:**
- 16:9 widescreen format
- Safe zone: 10% margin on all sides

**Title Area:**
- Top 15% of slide
- Background band option: Light gray (#F5F5F5)

**Content Area:**
- 70% of slide height
- Balanced left-right spacing

**Footer Area:**
- Bottom 5% of slide

---

### **Visual Design Principles**

**1. Hierarchy**
- Most important: Largest, boldest, positioned top-left
- Supporting: Medium size, positioned center
- Details: Smallest, positioned bottom/right

**2. Contrast**
- Dark text on light backgrounds
- Accent colors for emphasis
- 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum (WCAG AA)

**3. Alignment**
- Consistent left-alignment for text
- Center-alignment for visuals
- Grid-based layout

**4. White Space**
- Don't fill every pixel
- Breathing room around visuals
- Separate sections clearly

**5. Consistency**
- Same fonts throughout
- Consistent color usage
- Matching visual styles

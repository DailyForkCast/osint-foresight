Summer school in Suzhou 2025

As part of the cooperation between the University of Milan and Xi'an Jiaotong - Liverpool University in Suzhou (XJTLU), 10 positions are made available for students enrolled in IPLE, SIE, GOV and REL to attend one of the following summer schools organized by XJTLU for summer 2025:


1. Doing Business in Changing China
Dates: June 30-July 11, 2025
Site: Doing Business in a Changing China: 2025 Summer School - Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University


2. Chinese Language
Dates: July 7-August 1, 2025
Site: Chinese Language Summer School - Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University


Selected students will be exempt from paying summer school tuition fees, while they will be responsible for their travel and living expenses in China.


To apply, an email should be sent to internationalaffairsoffice.dilhps@unimi.it by March 21, 2025, (12.00), attaching the following documents:

  • screenshot of Unimia page showing matriculation number, exams and GPA
  • English Language certification of level B2 or higher issued by SLAM or other appropriate body (the list of certifications recognized by the University is available here Other foreign languages: tests and courses | Università degli Studi di Milano Statale)
  • brief letter of motivation, stating which of the two summer schools you intend to apply for and justifying your interest considering your course of study.

Applications will be accepted in order of submission until all available places are filled up. Should you need further information, please contact Marta Marchetti (internationalaffairsoffice.dilhps@unimi.it) and Simone Dossi (simone.dossi@unimi.it).

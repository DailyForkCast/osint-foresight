**TED Processor Design:**

**Our Flawed Definition:**

## FAILSAFE PROTOCOLS NEEDED

### 1. **Multi-Perspective Analysis**
- Always ask: "What does Company X SELL TO China?" not just "buy from"
- Include commercial relationships, not just government contracts
- Map corporate ownership structures completely

### 2. **Source Diversification Requirements**
- Minimum 5 different data source types per major finding
- Include: Procurement + Commercial + Research + Corporate + Trade
- Cross-validate with industry publications and news

### 3. **Red Team Validation**
- Every "protected" or "successful" finding must be challenged
- Assign someone to argue the opposite case
- Require evidence of active protection measures, not just absence of exposure

### 4. **Corporate Structure Mapping**
- Full ownership tree for any company deemed "protected"
- Technology sharing pathways between subsidiaries
- Geographic firewalls and their effectiveness

### 5. **Technology Criticality Assessment**
- Define specific technology capabilities at risk
- Assess dual-use potential of civil products
- Evaluate intelligence value to adversaries

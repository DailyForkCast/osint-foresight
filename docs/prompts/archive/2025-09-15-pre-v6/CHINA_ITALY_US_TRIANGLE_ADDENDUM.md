# China-Italy-US Triangle: Critical Intelligence Focus

**PRIORITY DIRECTIVE**: While US-Italy technology overlaps are important, the PRIMARY intelligence requirement is identifying **China's exploitation pathways through Italy to access US research, technology, and data**.

## Core Intelligence Questions

### 1. The Triangle Dynamic
- **Primary Focus**: How is China using Italy as a backdoor to US technology?
- **Secondary**: What Italian entities serve as conduits for Chinese access?
- **Tertiary**: Which US technologies are at risk through Italian intermediaries?

## Critical Collection Requirements

### China-Italy Technology Bridges
Look for:
- Chinese investments in Italian tech companies that have US contracts
- Joint ventures between Chinese and Italian firms in critical sectors
- Chinese researchers at Italian institutions collaborating with US entities
- Belt and Road Initiative (BRI) projects creating dependencies
- Chinese acquisition of Italian companies with US technology access

### Leonardo DRS China Connections (Example Framework)
For Leonardo DRS specifically, investigate:
1. **Direct China Links**:
   - Any Leonardo S.p.A. contracts with Chinese entities
   - Leonardo subsidiaries operating in China
   - Technology transfer agreements with Chinese firms
   - Chinese nationals employed at Leonardo facilities

2. **Indirect Pathways**:
   - Leonardo partners that also work with China
   - Supply chain components sourced from China
   - Research collaborations with dual-use potential
   - Italian government agreements that could compromise Leonardo

3. **Risk Indicators**:
   - Leonardo technologies that China seeks (night vision, EW, radar)
   - Cyber intrusions targeting Leonardo systems
   - Personnel with connections to Chinese institutions
   - Investment attempts by Chinese-linked entities

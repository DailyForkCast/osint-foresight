## 4. FALSE POSITIVE INCIDENT & LESSONS LEARNED

### The "NIO Problem"
- **Initial Finding:** 182,008 NIO contracts
- **Reality:** Substring matching error ("nio" in Antonio, union, senior)
- **Actual:** ~0 NIO automotive contracts
- **Lesson:** Always use word boundary validation

### Quality Control Improvements Implemented
1. Enhanced pattern matching with word boundaries
2. Statistical anomaly detection (>50% concentration = red flag)
3. Entity validation against known companies
4. Temporal consistency checking

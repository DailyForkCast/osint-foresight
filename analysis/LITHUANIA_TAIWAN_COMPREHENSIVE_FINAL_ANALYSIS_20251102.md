## Lag-Adjusted Impact Assessment

### Publication Lag Timelines

**Typical Research Project:**
- Grant submission: Month 0
- Grant award: Month 6-12
- Research execution: Month 12-36
- Paper writing: Month 36-42
- Peer review: Month 42-48
- Publication: **Month 48 (4 years)**

**Lithuania-China Crisis Timeline:**
- **July 2021:** Taiwan office announced
- **August 2021:** Ambassador recalled, sanctions imposed
- **Late 2021:** New collaborations frozen
- **2022-2023:** No new project initiations (or severely reduced)
- **2024:** Projects from 2021 still publishing (initiated pre-crisis)
- **2025:** Projects from 2022-2023 publishing (POST-crisis) ← **IMPACT VISIBLE**

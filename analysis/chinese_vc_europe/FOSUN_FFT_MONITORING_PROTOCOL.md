## VI. Analytical Framework

### A. Technology Transfer Indicators Analysis

When assessing potential technology transfer, evaluate:

1. **Access:** Does FFT technology move physically/digitally to China?
2. **Intent:** Is there evidence of deliberate transfer vs. normal business?
3. **Capability:** Does recipient have capability to absorb/apply technology?
4. **Impact:** Could transferred technology enhance PRC military capabilities?
5. **Control:** Are German/EU export controls being circumvented?

### B. Comparative Baseline

**Establish Normal Operations Baseline:**
- FFT's pre-acquisition (pre-2019) technology development pace
- Historical patent filing patterns
- Typical customer base and geographic focus
- Standard business practices in German automation industry

**Deviation Analysis:**
- Compare post-acquisition activity to baseline
- Identify statistically significant changes
- Distinguish Fosun influence from industry trends

### C. Alternative Hypotheses

Always consider alternative explanations before concluding technology transfer:

- **H1:** Changes are normal post-acquisition integration (innocent)
- **H2:** Changes reflect Chinese market expansion (commercial, not military)
- **H3:** Changes driven by German market conditions, not Chinese interests
- **H4:** Observed indicators are technology transfer for military purposes (threat)

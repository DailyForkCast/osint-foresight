## Executive Summary

**CRITICAL GAP IDENTIFIED:** Original analysis focused on **traditional Chinese VC firms** (Sequoia China, GGV, Hillhouse, etc.) but did **NOT** fully analyze **Corporate Venture Capital (CVC) arms** of major Chinese technology companies.

**Assessment:** These CVC arms represent **different investment pattern** from traditional VCs:

- **Often larger deals** ($200M-700M range for Ant Financial acquisitions)

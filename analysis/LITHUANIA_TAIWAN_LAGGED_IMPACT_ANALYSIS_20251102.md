## Publication Lag Fundamentals

### Typical Research-to-Publication Timeline

**Grant Application → Publication:** 2-4 years
- Grant submission: Month 0
- Grant award: Month 6-12
- Research execution: Month 12-36
- Writing & submission: Month 36-42
- Peer review & revision: Month 42-48
- Publication: Month 48

**Ongoing Project → Publication:** 1-2 years
- Research already underway at crisis time
- Writing & submission: Month 6-12
- Peer review: Month 12-18
- Publication: Month 18-24

**Impact on Lithuania-China Crisis (July 2021):**
- **2021 publications:** Projects initiated 2019-2020 (PRE-crisis)
- **2022 publications:** Projects initiated 2020-early 2021 (MOSTLY pre-crisis)
- **2023 publications:** Projects initiated late 2020-mid 2021 (MIXED)
- **2024 publications:** Projects initiated 2021-2022 (POST-crisis)
- **2025 publications:** Projects initiated 2022-2023 (POST-crisis)

## Lagged Impact Interpretation

### Phase 1: Crisis Year (2021)
- **Observed:** -3.5% decline (226 → 218)
- **Interpretation:** No immediate impact (expected)
- **Reason:** 2021 publications from 2019-2020 research projects

### Phase 2: Early Post-Crisis (2022)
- **Observed:** -8.3% decline (218 → 200)
- **Interpretation:** Modest decline begins
- **Reason:** Some late-2020 projects may have been affected, but most were already underway

### Phase 3: Backlog Peak (2023)
- **Observed:** +30% spike (200 → 260) **[CRITICAL YEAR]**
- **Interpretation:** Publication backlog clearing + late pre-crisis projects
- **Possible explanations:**
  1. **Backlog clearing:** 2020-early 2021 projects delayed by COVID-19 pandemic published in 2023
  2. **"Rush to publish":** Researchers pushed existing collaborations to publication before expecting full freeze
  3. **Pipeline effect:** Large cohort of projects initiated in peak year 2020 (+45.8%) publishing 2-3 years later
  4. **Delayed indexing:** OpenAlex may have batch-indexed 2023 publications later

### Phase 4: True Crisis Impact (2024-2025)
- **Observed:**
  - 2024: -21.5% decline (260 → 204)
  - 2025: -29.4% decline (204 → 144, partial year)
- **Interpretation:** **THIS IS THE REAL CRISIS IMPACT**
- **Reason:** Projects initiated AFTER July 2021 crisis now publishing
- **Evidence:**
  - New Lithuania-China collaborations likely stopped or severely reduced after August 2021
  - 2024 publications represent projects initiated in 2021-2022 (post-crisis)
  - 2025 publications represent projects initiated in 2022-2023 (deep post-crisis)

## Final Revised Narrative

**Claim:** Lithuania-China research dropped 89.3% immediately after crisis

**Reality:** Lithuania-China research showing ~17-20% decline 2.5-3.5 years after crisis

**Why the difference:**
1. **Fabricated numbers** (1,209 → 129 was never real)
2. **Publication lag ignored** (crisis was July 2021, impact visible 2024-2025)
3. **Pipeline effect** (2023 spike from pre-crisis projects completing)

**What actually happened:**
- July 2021: Diplomatic crisis
- 2021-2023: Pre-crisis research pipeline continues publishing (260 works in 2023)
- 2024-2025: New collaborations frozen, publications decline (-21.5% in 2024)
- Ongoing: Impact still unfolding, likely stabilizing at 15-20% below pre-crisis baseline

**Lesson:** Publication lag is CRITICAL for assessing research collaboration impacts. Immediate data misleading. Need 3-5 year window for true assessment.

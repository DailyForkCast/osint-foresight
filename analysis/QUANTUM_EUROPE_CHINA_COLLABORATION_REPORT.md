### Data Sources

### Analysis Approach

1. **Quantum Research Identification**: Works identified through comprehensive keyword matching:
   - Keywords: quantum, quantum computing, quantum sensing, quantum communication, quantum cryptography, qubit, quantum entanglement, quantum information
   - Searched in: titles and abstracts

2. **Collaboration Detection**: China collaborations identified through co-authorship analysis:
   - Works with authors from both European and Chinese institutions
   - Country affiliations determined via openalex_work_authors table

3. **Risk Assessment Framework**: Multi-factor scoring system:
   - **Collaboration Volume** (0-3 points): Absolute number of China co-authorships
   - **Collaboration Rate** (0-2 points): Percentage of quantum works involving China
   - **Research Capacity** (0-2 points): Total quantum research output indicating technology transfer potential

**Risk Level Thresholds**:
- **CRITICAL** (Score ≥5): Immediate enhanced protocols required
- **HIGH** (Score 3-4): Enhanced monitoring and screening necessary
- **MODERATE** (Score 2): Standard protocols with periodic review
- **LOW** (Score 1): Basic monitoring sufficient
- **MINIMAL** (Score 0): Standard research practices adequate

### Limitations

- **Co-authorship Coverage**: Not all collaboration types captured (consulting, equipment sharing, etc.)
- **Publication Lag**: Recent collaborations may not yet be published

- **Keyword Matching**: May miss some quantum research with specialized terminology
- **Commercial Research**: Industry and classified government research not included
- **Risk Assessment**: Indicative only; detailed institutional reviews recommended

## 3. ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY SUPPLY CHAINS

### Critical Minerals Dominance (2024 Data)

#### China's Market Control:
- **Rare Earth Elements:** >90% of global refining capacity
- **Graphite:** >90% of global refining capacity
- **Lithium:** ~60% of global processing
- **Cobalt:** ~60% of global processing
- **Nickel:** 90% of 2024 capacity additions (China + Indonesia)

#### Supply Chain Concentration:
- Top 3 refining nations control 86% of market (up from 82% in 2020)
- China dominant in all minerals except nickel (Indonesia leads)
- Geographic concentration increased across nearly all critical minerals

### Export Controls and Geopolitical Weaponization

#### China's Export Restrictions (2023-2024):
- **Germanium and Gallium:** Export controls implemented 2023
- **Rare Earth Elements:** New controls in response to US tariffs
- **Strategic Impact:** Creating vulnerabilities in clean energy, defense, technology

#### Market Impact:
- **Lithium:** Prices fell 80% from 2022 peak
- **Investment:** Global critical mineral investment growth slowed to 5% (2024) from 14% (2023)
- **Demand Growth:** Lithium demand up 30% in 2024

## 4. SEMICONDUCTOR SUPPLY CHAIN DISRUPTION

### US Export Controls Timeline:
- **October 2022:** Initial major controls
- **October 2023:** Controls tightened
- **December 2024:** Additional restrictions
- **March 2025:** Trump administration blacklisted dozens of Chinese entities

### TSMC Restrictions:
- **2024:** US revoked special export license for Nanjing Fab 16
- **Impact:** Disrupts 16nm and 28nm operations by end 2025
- **Customer Shift:** Chinese demand moving to SMIC and HuaHong

### ASML and Netherlands Controls:
- **Equipment Banned:** NXT:2000i and higher DUV lithography (2024)
- **Revenue Impact:** China contributed 29% of ASML revenue (2023) vs 14% (2022)
- **Doubled Revenue:** Despite restrictions, China revenue share doubled

### China's Response and Progress:
- **SMIC Advances:** First Chinese company to achieve 7nm (2022), reportedly 5nm (2023)
- **Huawei Breakthrough:** Mate 60 Pro with indigenous 5G modem (2023)
- **Import Surge:** Q3 2023 chip equipment imports up 93% to $8.75B
- **Industry Revenue:** $179.5B in 2023, 7.31% CAGR projected 2023-2027

### Technology Limitations:
- **3nm Barrier:** SMIC cannot reach 3nm with existing DUV tools
- **5nm Limit:** Industry consensus on DUV technology ceiling

## Conclusion

This institutional analysis reveals **systematic and extensive China engagement** across European quantum research, with several institutions showing **critical levels of collaboration** (>40% of publications).

The **Czech Academy of Sciences Institute of Physics** represents the highest strategic concern with 61.9% China collaboration rate, followed by Italian facilities at Gran Sasso (57-79%) and German Max Planck Institute for Physics (40%).

While most European institutions maintain "normal" collaboration rates of 6-8%, the **absolute volume of quantum technology transfer** through academic publications raises significant strategic concerns for European technological sovereignty.

**The data clearly demonstrates that China has established deep research partnerships with Europe's leading quantum institutions across all critical quantum technology domains.**

# Italian Universities Working with Chinese Military Universities
**Analysis Date:** October 17, 2025
**Scope:** Primary and secondary level collaborations with China's "Seven Sons of National Defense"
**Data Sources:** CORDIS, ASPI, OpenAlex

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This analysis identifies Italian academic institutions collaborating with China's "Seven Sons of National Defense" universities - institutions with direct ties to China's defense industry and military research programs. Collaborations are categorized as:

- **PRIMARY LEVEL:** Direct research collaborations through EU Framework Programs (CORDIS) and academic publications (OpenAlex)
- **SECONDARY LEVEL:** Corporate training programs, technology partnerships (ASPI data)

**Key Finding:** At least **15 distinct Italian universities and research institutions** have documented collaborations with Chinese military-linked universities across multiple channels.

---

## CHINA'S "SEVEN SONS OF NATIONAL DEFENSE"

These seven universities were established by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology specifically to support China's defense industrial complex:

1. **Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT)** - Weapons systems, aerospace
2. **Beihang University (BUAA)** - Aeronautics, astronautics, missiles
3. **Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT)** - Aerospace, robotics, satellites
4. **Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU)** - Aviation, space, marine technology
5. **Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA)** - Aerospace engineering
6. **Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NUST)** - Weapons, ammunition
7. **Harbin Engineering University (HEU)** - Naval architecture, nuclear engineering
